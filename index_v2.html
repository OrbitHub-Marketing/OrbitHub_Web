LOCATION

Where modernism meets technology

In 2025, NovaLabs Space opens its first innovation cluster in the industrial precinct of Colonia Güell — a former textile factory founded in 1890 by industrialist Eusebi Güell that once stood as a pillar of Barcelona's growth and prosperity. Renowned for its rich Modernist architecture, originally envisioned by Antoni Gaudí, this site is now the stage for a new high-tech era.

The Industrial Heritage of Colonia Güell

The Industrial precinct of Colonia Güell is a historic textile factory complex located on the outskirts of Barcelona. Conceived to integrate industry with innovation, it exemplified the vision for industrial colonies in Catalonia during the late 19th and early 20th centuries". Renowned for its Modernist architecture, it remains a symbol of industrial heritage and visionary planning.

About us

NovaLabs Space was founded by a team of scientists based in Barcelona, with years of international experience in Space, Deeptech, and startup ecosystems. We aim to solve a key challenge: the lack of accessible, high-quality laboratories and office areas for small R&D startups.

Our mission is to create an innovation-driven environment tailored to the needs of early-stage Deeptech and Space ventures — providing flexibility, infrastructure, and a collaborative spirit.
