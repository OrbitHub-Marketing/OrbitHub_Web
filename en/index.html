A DEEPTECH INFRASTRUCTURE HUB

A dynamic environment built for R&D and Space startups to connect, innovate, and scale.

OrbitHub is a DeepTech infrastructure platform designed to support technological innovation. We provide photonics design & testing capabilities, together with access to advanced laboratory and cleanroom facilities for industrial collaborators and startups developing next-generation DeepTech and space technologies. OrbitHub aims to support the transition from research and prototyping toward industrial development and real-world deployment.

Laboratory area

Advanced laboratory and cleanroom areas for R&D activities, with controlled technical environments, including semiconductor fabrication equipment and specialized photonics fabrication tooling.

Collaborative workspace

Modern office space adjacent to the laboratories and cleanroom areas for lab-related activities and business operations, with open desks, private offices, meeting rooms and phone booths.

Services

Photonics design & testing capabilities, including high-frequency test beds, together with technical and operational support for R&D teams, industrial collaborations and grant development.

LOCATION

Where modernism meets technology

In 2025, OrbitHub opens its first innovation cluster in the industrial precinct of Colonia Güell — a former textile factory founded in 1890 by industrialist Eusebi Güell that once stood as a pillar of Barcelona's growth and prosperity. Renowned for its rich Modernist architecture, originally envisioned by Antoni Gaudí, this site is now the stage for a new high-tech era.

The Industrial Heritage of Colonia Güell

The industrial precinct of Colonia Güell is a historic textile factory complex located on the outskirts of Barcelona. Conceived to integrate industry with innovation, it exemplified the vision for industrial colonies in Catalonia during the late 19th and early 20th centuries. Renowned for its Modernist architecture, it remains a symbol of industrial heritage and visionary planning.

OrbitHub – Recinte Industrial Colonia Güell

Discover OrbitHub's innovation cluster – a hub of high-tech laboratories, offices, and services – located in the historic industrial precinct of Colonia Güell. The campus is fully enclosed, with video surveillance and a security gatehouse at the entrance. Just 30 minutes from Barcelona’s city center by public transport.

About us

OrbitHub was founded by a team of scientists with years of international experience in Deeptech and Space startup ecosystems. We aim to solve a key challenge: the lack of accessible, high-quality laboratories and office areas for small R&D startups.

Our mission is to create an innovative environment tailored to the needs of early-stage Deeptech and Space ventures — providing flexibility, infrastructure, and a collaborative spirit.
